Welcome to Coddled Software

Video games have the potential to revolutionize adult education by offering immersive environments for studying difficult subjects.

Very few modern video games have lived up to this lofty goal, and Coddled Software is passionate about creating high-quality games that teach skills while entertaining. The dream is that these games will be at least 50% entertainment, with enough educational content that users will feel that they are accomplishing something substantial.
